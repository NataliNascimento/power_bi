# 📊 Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project presents a sales performance dashboard developed in Power BI, focused on revenue analysis, sales distribution, and business insights.

## 📁 Dataset
- Source: Course dataset
- Format: Excel

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze total revenue and sales performance
- Track monthly revenue evolution
- Identify top customers and sales representatives
- Understand sales distribution across states and categories

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

### 🏠 Home
- Report title
- Navigation buttons to access different views

### 📈 Overview Page (Hidden)
- Date filter
- Total revenue and key metrics
- Revenue by year
- Monthly revenue evolution
- Revenue by state (UF)
- Top 5 customers by revenue
- Quantity sold by category
- Top 5 customers by quantity

### 🔍 Detailed View (Hidden)
- Filters: year, month, category, sales representative, state, and customer
- Category and quantity analysis
- Tables with:
  - Top sales representatives
  - States with highest sales

## 📈 Key Insights
- Revenue shows variation over time with identifiable growth patterns
- Certain states concentrate a significant portion of total sales
- A small group of customers represents a large share of revenue
- Sales performance varies significantly across categories and regions

- Creating multi-page dashboards with navigation
- Applying filters for dynamic analysis
- Designing user-friendly layouts in Power BI
- Extracting business insights from sales data
